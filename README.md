## Notes

The notebook is designed to use Google Colab for the fine-tuning of the model, to minimize the environment set-up complexity during the lecture.

To use this notebook, download the `camp2025_nn.ipynb` file and open https://colab.research.google.com/, import the notebook and follow the instructions of the notebook.

You will need to prepare the dataset on your own according to instructions within the notebook. The dataset should be uploaded to your Google Drive for the Colab notebook to access. If your organization (e.g. school-provided Google account) do not permit this, you may want to use your personal account.
